
# 📡 CPE Operations & Service Management Dashboard

## 🎯 Objective

Manage and monitor Customer Premises Equipment (CPE) operations, providing full visibility over installations, removals, repairs, and service activities across partner and third-party providers.

## 🧩 Data Model

![data-model](../../assets/model-cpe.png)

This model follows a star schema structure designed for operational analysis and performance tracking.

---

## 🧠 Business Context

This dashboard supports the management of telecom service operations related to Customer Premises Equipment (CPE).

It provides a centralized view of service orders (OS), covering installation, removal, repair, and maintenance activities executed by partner and third-party providers.

## 🔧 Tools & Techniques

- Power BI

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

- Total Service Orders (OS)  
- Installations vs Removals  
- Open vs Closed Repairs  
- Scheduling Performance  
- SLA Compliance  
- Equipment Lifecycle Tracking  

---

## 💡 Key Features

- 📦 CPE lifecycle management (installation → removal)  
- 🔧 Repair tracking (open and historical)  
- 📅 Scheduling and customer appointments analysis  
- 🤝 Performance by partner and third-party providers  
- 📊 Equipment model tracking  
- 📌 Status tracking of service orders  

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Identification of delays in installations and repairs  
- Performance differences across providers  
- High volume of rework or repeated service orders  
- Bottlenecks in scheduling and execution  

---

## 🚀 Business Impact

- Improved control over telecom field operations  
- Better visibility of service lifecycle  
- Enhanced partner performance management  
- Faster identification of operational issues
